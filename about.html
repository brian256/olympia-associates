Our Story

I've been doing this work for twenty years. Olympia & Associates is where it landed.

My name is Brian Ssennoga. I'm the founder of Olympia & Associates — a consulting practice based in Edmonton, Alberta. Before Edmonton, there was Kampala. Before Kampala, there was a career built across technology, leadership, and the systems that shape how people build lives and organisations.

Artwork: Keanna Suubi

The beginning

Three chapters. One through-line.

The practice didn't start with a business plan. It started with a pattern I kept observing — in organisations, in teams, in the people I worked alongside: the gap between what someone is capable of and what the systems around them make visible.

A first-time manager promoted without a framework. A nonprofit with a mission and no structure to make it scalable. A family arriving in a new country with credentials that don't translate. A business owner with a vision and a plan that hasn't been pressure-tested. A student with genuine ability and a course selection that quietly closes the doors she hasn't noticed yet.

In every case, the problem isn't the person. It's the gap between what they're building and the structure that would make it real. That's the gap Olympia & Associates works in.

The evidence

The work speaks for itself.

The track record behind this practice spans three chapters — a management coaching practice in Kampala, a technology company that served 780,000+ refugees, and an early Edmonton chapter with engagements across four practice areas.

The full account — case studies, outcomes, credibility anchors — is documented on a single page. It is an honest record. Where testimonials are pending confirmation, that is noted. Where engagements didn't proceed to contract, that is noted too.

On the practice

This is early-stage. Deliberately.

Olympia & Associates is a registered Alberta trade name. No investors. No partners. One consultant — doing the work, building the track record, and being transparent about where the practice is in its growth.

The depth behind the work is real. The Edmonton client roster is early. The social proof infrastructure — testimonials, published case studies, a visible track record — is being built now, not retrospectively.

If you engage Olympia & Associates today, you're working directly with Brian Ssennoga. That's a deliberate design, not a limitation.
